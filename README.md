# Credit Card Analytics Dashboard

A Power BI dashboard project for analyzing credit card transactions and customer data.

---

# Dashboards

## Transaction Report
- Revenue
- Total Interest
- Total Amount
- Revenue by Expenditure Type
- Education Level
- Customer Job
- Card Category
- Use Chip

---

## Customer Report
- Revenue
- Total Interest
- Income
- Revenue by Week
- Revenue by Age Group
- Revenue by Marital Status
- Revenue by Income Group
- Revenue by Education
- Revenue by Customer Job
- Top 5 States

---

# Tools Used
- Power BI
- MySQL
